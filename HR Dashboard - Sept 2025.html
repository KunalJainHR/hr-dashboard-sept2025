Case Study: Operational Disruption Summary

Attrition Due to Managerial Mismanagement of Leave

A highly valuable employee was granted a *15-day informed leave* by the Hotel Manager (HM). Due to *lack of communication and extended absence* beyond the agreed period, the HM hired a replacement to mitigate operational risk.

Upon the original employee's intimation to return, *no open roles were available*, leading to the employee's departure. This incident highlights a severe failure in the leave management process and directly resulted in *avoidable attrition* due to poor communication between the HM and the absent employee.
